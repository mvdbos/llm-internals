Resources

Curated, high-trust resources for learning about LLM internals and quantization.

Primary sources (text)

ResourceWhy it's good
ML Journey: Quantized LLMs Explained (Q4 vs Q8 vs FP16) Comprehensive, practical guide with decision framework. Good analogies (ruler). Covers group-wise, asymmetric quantization.
Demystifying LLM Quantization Suffixes Explains GGUF suffixes (Q4_K_M, Q8_0, IQ3_XXS, etc.) and what the letters mean. Practical download decisions.
LLM Hardware Quantization Guide Focused on VRAM formulas and hardware tradeoffs. Good for "which quant fits my GPU?"
Sebastian Raschka: Recent Developments in LLM Architectures Deep technical but very clear. Covers layer types, KV sharing, MoE.

Primary sources (video)

ResourceWhy it's good
3Blue1Brown: Transformers (Deep Learning Ch. 5) The gold standard visual explanation of how data flows through transformer layers. Grant Sanderson.
Andrej Karpathy: Intro to Large Language Models 1-hour talk. Covers the full stack from training to inference, including quantization pragmatics.

ResourceWhy it's good
llama.cpp GGUF quantization types The source of truth for what Q4_K_M, Q8_0, IQ3_XXS actually mean.
